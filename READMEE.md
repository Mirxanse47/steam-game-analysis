# 🎮 Steam Game Data Analysis

## 📊 Overview
This project analyzes the relationship between game price, playtime, and player satisfaction using Steam data.

## 🎯 Objective
To understand whether more expensive or longer games provide better player experiences.

---

## 📁 Dataset
- Total games analyzed: 5,594
- Story-like games: 4,444

---

## 🔍 Analysis
- Price vs Playtime
- Playtime vs Rating
- Price vs Rating
- Value metric (playtime per price)

---

## 📈 Results

- Price vs Playtime → 0.32 (weak relationship)
- Playtime vs Rating → 0.10 (very weak)
- Price vs Rating → 0.22 (weak)

---

## 💡 Conclusion

Players do not value games based on how long or how expensive they are,  
but on how good the experience is.
